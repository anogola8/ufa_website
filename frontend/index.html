Who We Are

About UFA

United Future Alliance is a grassroots movement committed to transforming communities through civic education and sustainable development.

Vision

A united, prosperous society where every citizen actively participates in sustainable development and democratic governance.

Mission

To educate, sensitize, and advocate for policy influence while mobilizing membership for collective action aligned with Article 7.

Core Values

Integrity, Inclusivity, Transparency, Unity, Resilience, and Accountability in all our operations and engagements.

Our Structure
